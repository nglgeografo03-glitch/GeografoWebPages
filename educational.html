Digital Kindness & Honesty

Learn to be good and honest online

What is Online Kindness?

Online kindness means treating people with respect and care when you are on the internet. It means:

  • Being polite in messages and comments
  • Helping others when they ask for advice
  • Not spreading harmful or false information

What is Honesty in Digital Creation?

Honesty in digital creation means you create or share content that is true and original. It means:

  • Not copying someone else's work without permission
  • Giving credit to people who helped or inspired you
  • Not lying or cheating online

Tips to Be Good Online

  • Think before you post anything.
  • Be respectful even if you disagree.
  • Always check facts before sharing information.
  • Encourage and support others positively.
  • Follow rules and guidelines of websites and platforms.

Remember:

The internet is like a big community. Your words and actions online can make a difference. Be kind and honest, and you help make the digital world better for everyone!
